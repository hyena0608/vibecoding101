## Choosing Your First AI Coding Tool

With so many AI coding tools available, picking the right one can feel overwhelming. This guide compares the top 5 tools specifically for people with zero coding experience.

We rated each tool on three criteria:
- **Ease of use** — How quickly can a complete beginner get results?
- **Power** — How complex of an app can you build?
- **Cost** — How much does it cost to get started?

## 1. v0 by Vercel

**Best for: Instant visual results**

v0 turns text descriptions and images into working web interfaces. Paste a screenshot of a design, describe what you want, and get a working React component in seconds.

- Ease of use: ★★★★★
- Power: ★★★☆☆
- Cost: Free tier available

<Callout type="tip">
v0 is perfect for your first vibe coding experience. Type "a pricing page with three tiers" and watch it generate a complete page in under 10 seconds.
</Callout>

## 2. Cursor

**Best for: Building complete applications**

Cursor is a code editor with AI built in. It's more powerful than v0 but has a slightly steeper learning curve. You can build full applications — backends, databases, everything.

- Ease of use: ★★★★☆
- Power: ★★★★★
- Cost: Free tier, Pro at $20/month

## 3. Claude Code

**Best for: Conversational app building**

Claude Code by Anthropic lets you build apps through a natural conversation. Describe your project, and Claude writes the code, creates files, and even runs tests.

- Ease of use: ★★★★☆
- Power: ★★★★★
- Cost: Included with Claude subscription

## 4. Bolt.new

**Best for: Quick browser-based projects**

Bolt runs entirely in your browser — no installation needed. Describe what you want, and it creates a full-stack app you can preview and deploy immediately.

- Ease of use: ★★★★★
- Power: ★★★☆☆
- Cost: Free tier available

## 5. Replit Agent

**Best for: Learning while building**

Replit's AI agent builds apps for you while explaining what it's doing. Great if you want to eventually understand the code, not just generate it.

- Ease of use: ★★★★☆
- Power: ★★★★☆
- Cost: Free tier, paid plans from $15/month

## Our Recommendation

**Start with v0** to get instant gratification and build confidence. Then move to **Cursor** when you want to build something more complex. Use **Claude Code** when you want a conversational experience for planning and building larger projects.

The best tool is the one you actually use — so pick one and start building today!
