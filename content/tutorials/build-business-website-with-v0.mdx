## What You'll Build

A complete business website with:
- Home page (hero, features, CTA)
- About page (team story, mission)
- Services/pricing page
- Contact page with a form
- Responsive navigation
- Professional design

This is the #1 thing people want to build with AI tools — and v0 makes it the fastest.

## Before You Start

- A web browser
- A [v0.dev](https://v0.dev) account (free)
- 15 minutes

No downloads. No setup. Just open v0 and start typing.

<Step number={1} title="Create the Home Page">

Go to [v0.dev](https://v0.dev) and type:

```
Create a home page for a digital marketing agency called "BrightWave."

Hero section: Large headline "Grow Your Business Online" with a subtitle
about data-driven marketing strategies. Primary button "Get a Free Audit"
and secondary button "See Our Work." Background with a subtle gradient.

Features: 4 cards in a 2x2 grid:
- SEO Optimization (search icon)
- Social Media Marketing (megaphone icon)
- Content Strategy (pencil icon)
- Analytics & Reporting (chart icon)

Stats bar: "150+ Clients", "98% Retention", "3x Average ROI"

CTA section: "Ready to grow?" with email input and submit button.

Modern, clean design. Blue as primary color, dark navy accents.
```

</Step>

<Step number={2} title="Create the About Page">

In the same v0 project, add:

```
Create an About page for BrightWave:

Hero: "Our Story" headline with a short paragraph about being founded
in 2024 by marketers frustrated with agencies that overpromise.

Mission section: "We believe in transparent, measurable marketing
that actually moves the needle."

Values: 3 columns — "Data First", "Radical Transparency", "Results Over Vanity"

Team section: 4 team cards with placeholder avatar circles (no real photos),
name, role, and a short bio. Use abstract geometric avatar placeholders.

Timeline: Founded 2024 → First 50 clients 2025 → 150+ clients 2026
```

<Callout type="tip">
Always use "placeholder avatars" or "abstract geometric avatars" — never ask AI to generate photos of real-looking people.
</Callout>

</Step>

<Step number={3} title="Create the Services Page">

```
Create a Services/Pricing page:

Three pricing tiers:
- Starter ($999/mo): SEO audit, 2 blog posts, monthly report
- Growth ($2,499/mo): Full SEO, social media management, weekly reports
- Enterprise (Custom): Everything + dedicated strategist, daily reporting

Each tier in a card with a feature checklist and CTA button.
Growth tier highlighted as "Most Popular."

Below pricing: FAQ section with 5 questions about contracts, results
timeline, reporting, and what "data-driven" means.
```

</Step>

<Step number={4} title="Create the Contact Page">

```
Create a Contact page:

Contact form with: Name, Email, Company, Budget range (dropdown:
Under $1k, $1k-$3k, $3k-$5k, $5k+), Message textarea, Submit button.

Below the form: Office address (123 Market St, SF), email
(hello@brightwave.com), phone.

Map placeholder (gray rectangle with "Map" text — don't embed
an actual Google Map).
```

</Step>

<Step number={5} title="Add Navigation">

```
Add a responsive navigation bar that works across all pages:
- BrightWave logo on the left
- Links: Home, About, Services, Contact
- "Get Started" button on the right (primary color)
- On mobile: hamburger menu that opens a slide-out nav
- Sticky header with blur background
```

</Step>

<Step number={6} title="Deploy">

Click **Deploy** in v0. You'll get a live URL in seconds.

To connect a custom domain later, you can transfer the project to Vercel and add your domain in the dashboard.

</Step>

## Customizing for Your Business

Replace "BrightWave" and "digital marketing agency" with your own business:

- **Restaurant**: Change features to Menu, Reservations, Catering, Events
- **Freelancer**: Change to Portfolio, Services, Testimonials, Hire Me
- **Local business**: Add location info, hours, Google Reviews section
- **Startup**: Focus on the product, add a demo video section

The structure works for any business — just swap the content.

## v0 vs Claude Code: When to Use Which?

| Need | Use |
|------|-----|
| Quick landing page or UI | **v0** — instant visual results |
| Multi-page site with navigation | **v0** — handles routing and layout |
| App with data, forms, backend | **Claude Code** — full-stack capability |
| Custom logic and APIs | **Claude Code** — writes server code |
| Just need it to look good fast | **v0** — design is its strength |
